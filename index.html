Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a trailer in Eagle Mountain?
Simple — pick your trailer above, click "Check Availability," choose your dates on our booking system, and confirm. The whole process takes under 5 minutes. No phone call required, though we're always available at (385) 269-0712.
Are you really open 24/7?
Yes. You can book online any time of day or night. Our AI-assisted phone system handles calls and texts 24/7. If you need a trailer Sunday evening or early Monday morning before the work week, we've got you covered — unlike other rental companies in the area.
What do I need to rent a trailer?
A valid driver's license, proof of current auto insurance, and a vehicle with the correct hitch and plug for the trailer you're renting. All of our trailers use a bumper pull (ball hitch) connection.
Do you deliver trailers?
Currently trailers are customer pick-up from our Eagle Mountain location. We serve customers from Eagle Mountain, Saratoga Springs, Lehi, American Fork, Cedar Fort, Fairfield, and surrounding Utah County communities.
What is the cheapest trailer rental in Eagle Mountain?
Our 7'×14' utility trailer starts at $40/day — one of the most competitive rates in Utah County. The dump trailer is $80/day and the 24ft enclosed trailer is $95/day. No hidden fees, no surprise charges.
Can my truck or SUV tow these trailers?
Most full-size trucks and SUVs can. Our utility trailer uses a 2" ball hitch with a flat 4-pin plug; the dump and enclosed trailers use a 2-5/16" ball with a 7-pin round plug. Check your vehicle's tow rating against the loaded trailer weight before you book.
Can I rent a trailer same-day or on a Sunday?
Yes. Because booking is online and we're open 24/7, you can reserve and pick up the same day — including Sundays, holidays, and after hours — as long as the trailer is available.
Do I need insurance to rent a trailer?
Yes. You'll need proof of current auto insurance along with a valid driver's license to rent any of our trailers.
