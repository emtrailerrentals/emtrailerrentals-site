Salt Lake County Trailer Rental FAQs

Do you serve Salt Lake County?
Yes — we serve the south end of Salt Lake County: West Jordan, Riverton, Herriman, South Jordan, Draper, and Bluffdale. We're based in Eagle Mountain, a 20–30 minute drive down Mountain View Corridor or Bangerter Highway, and many of our regular renters come from these cities.
How much does trailer rental cost for Salt Lake County renters?
The same flat rates as everyone: utility trailer $40/day, 14k dump trailer $80/day, and 24ft enclosed trailer $95/day. No mileage fees, no surcharges for where you live — you pick up in Eagle Mountain and pay the flat rate.
Why rent from Eagle Mountain instead of a Salt Lake City rental?
Three reasons our Salt Lake County customers give: flat transparent pricing, newer equipment, and 24/7 online booking. For anyone in the southwest valley, the drive down Mountain View Corridor is often faster than fighting traffic into Salt Lake proper.
What do I need to rent a trailer?
A valid driver's license, proof of current auto insurance, and a vehicle with the right hitch: 2" ball and flat-4 connector for the utility trailer; 2-5/16" ball and 7-pin connector for the dump and enclosed trailers.
