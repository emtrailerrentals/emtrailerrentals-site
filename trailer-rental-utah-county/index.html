Utah County Trailer Rental FAQs

How much does it cost to rent a trailer in Utah County?
Flat daily rates: a 7×14 utility trailer is $40/day, a 14k dump trailer is $80/day, and a 24ft enclosed trailer is $85/day. Weekly and monthly discounts are available, with no hidden fees.
Where in Utah County can I rent a trailer?
We're based in Eagle Mountain and serve all of Utah County — Saratoga Springs, Lehi, American Fork, Cedar Valley, Provo, Spanish Fork, Springville, Payson — plus nearby Salt Lake County cities (Herriman, Riverton, South Jordan, Draper, Bluffdale). Trailers are customer pickup from Eagle Mountain.
Can I rent on weekends or at night?
Yes — we're open 24/7. Booking is online, so you can reserve and pick up any hour of any day, including weekends and holidays, with no phone call needed.
What do I need to rent a trailer?
A valid driver's license, proof of current auto insurance, and a vehicle with the correct hitch and plug. The utility trailer uses a 2-inch ball and flat-4 connector; the dump and enclosed trailers use a 2-5/16-inch ball and 7-pin connector.
