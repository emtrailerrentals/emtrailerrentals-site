Utility Trailer FAQ

What is the cheapest trailer rental in Eagle Mountain?
Our utility trailer at $40/day is the most affordable option in Eagle Mountain and among the best rates in all of Utah County — with no hidden fees.
Will the utility trailer fit my ATV or side-by-side?
The 7'×14' deck fits most single ATVs and dirt bikes comfortably. Narrower side-by-sides (under 60") will fit as well. Wider UTV models may need our larger flatbed — call us at (385) 269-0712 to confirm your vehicle's dimensions.
What truck do I need to tow the utility trailer?
Any full-size pickup or mid-size truck with a 2" ball hitch and Flat 4 plug connection rated for at least 3,500 lbs. This includes most F-150s, Silverados, RAMs, Tacomas, and capable SUVs like a Suburban or Sequoia.
Can I rent the utility trailer for multiple days?
Absolutely. Multi-day rentals are $240/week or $900/month — just select your dates in the booking system and the price updates automatically.
