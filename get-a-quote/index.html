Trailer Rental Quote FAQ

How much does it cost to rent a trailer in Eagle Mountain?

Daily rates are $40 for the 7'×14' utility trailer, $80 for the 7×14 dump trailer, and $85 for the 24ft enclosed trailer. Weekly and monthly rates are lower per day — use the instant quote tool above to see your exact total.

Do weekly or monthly rentals cost less per day?

Yes. Weekly rates are $240 (utility), $500 (dump), and $510 (enclosed); monthly rates are $900 (utility), $1,200 (dump), and $1,500 (enclosed) — a significant discount versus the daily rate.

How fast will I get my quote?

Instantly. Pick a trailer and your dates and the price appears on screen. For custom or multi-trailer jobs, submit the custom quote form and we typically respond within the hour — our AI assistant follows up 24/7.

Can I book the trailer right after getting a quote?

Yes. Every quote includes a Book Now link straight to our live booking system, where you can confirm real-time availability and reserve in minutes — any time, day or night.
