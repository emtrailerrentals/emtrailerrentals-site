Privacy Policy

How Eagle Mountain Trailer Rental LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

HomePrivacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

Eagle Mountain Trailer Rental LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates emtrailerrentals.rent. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. By using our website you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us:

  • Contact details you submit — your name and phone number or email address when you use our quote or contact forms. These submissions are delivered to our inbox through our form provider (Web3Forms).
  • Automatic website data — like most websites, we use cookies and similar technologies (Google tag / Google Ads and analytics) to understand site traffic and measure the performance of our ads. This may include your device type, browser, pages visited, and general location.

Booking and payment are handled on our separate booking platform; any information you enter there is governed by that provider's own privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and follow up about your trailer rental or quote
  • To provide, operate, and improve our website and services
  • To measure and improve our advertising and understand how visitors use the site

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers that help us run the business — such as our form provider and Google's advertising and analytics services — and when required by law.

Cookies & Your Choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt out of personalized Google advertising at adssettings.google.com. Disabling cookies will not stop you from using the site.

Data Retention & Security

We keep the contact information you submit only as long as needed to respond to your request and for our normal business records, and we take reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the most recent version.
