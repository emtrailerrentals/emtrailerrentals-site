7'x14' Utility Trailer Pricing

Our open utility trailer is built for general hauling — furniture, yard waste, ATVs, landscaping materials, and more. $40/day, $240/week, $900/month. 1,945 lb capacity. Open ramp, 2" ball hitch, flat-4 plug.

24ft Enclosed Trailer Pricing

For loads that need to stay dry and secure, the 24ft enclosed trailer offers lockable, weather-protected hauling for larger jobs, moves, or equipment transport. $95/day, $570/week, $1,700/month. 6,130 lb capacity. Lockable and weather-protected, 2-5/16" ball hitch, round 7-pin plug.

7x14 Dump Trailer 14k Pricing

Our hydraulic dump trailer is designed for dirt, gravel, debris, and demolition material that needs to be hauled and unloaded fast. $80/day, $480/week, $1,200/month. 9,800 lb payload, 14,000 lb GVWR. Hydraulic dump, 2-5/16" ball hitch, round 7-pin plug. For a full breakdown of how much material that payload actually holds, see our dump trailer payload capacity guide.

How to Choose the Right Trailer for Your Budget

Comparing all three side by side: the utility trailer is the most affordable option for lighter loads, the enclosed trailer costs more but protects your cargo from weather and theft, and the dump trailer sits in the middle price-wise while offering the highest payload capacity and hydraulic unloading. All three are bumper-pull and available for pickup only from our Eagle Mountain, UT location. For guidance on multi-day projects, see our weekly vs. daily rate breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions
