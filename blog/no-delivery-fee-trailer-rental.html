Skip the Delivery Fee: Pickup-Only Trailer Rental Done Right

When you pick up and return the trailer yourself, there's no delivery charge to pay — just a straightforward price and a trailer that's ready when you are.

HomeBlogNo Delivery Fees

By Eagle Mountain Trailer Rental LLC · Updated July 2026

Some trailer rental setups quote a low daily rate, then add extra fees before you ever hitch up. Eagle Mountain Trailer Rental LLC does it differently: we don't offer delivery at all, which means there's no delivery fee to tack onto your bill in the first place. You pick up the trailer from our Eagle Mountain, UT location and bring it back yourself.

What "Pickup-Only" Actually Means

We want to be upfront about this: pickup-only means you are responsible for getting the trailer to and from our Eagle Mountain location. We do not offer delivery or drop-off service in any form, at any price. If you need a trailer brought to your driveway or job site, our setup won't work for you, and we'd rather you know that before you book than find out after.

For everyone who can make the drive, though, this model has a real upside: it's one less line item on your invoice.

Why This Keeps Pricing Simple

Delivery service — where it's offered at all in this industry — isn't free to provide. Someone has to drive the trailer to you, drop it off, and often come back to pick it up again later. Because we don't run a delivery fleet or offer that service, that cost simply doesn't exist in our pricing. What you see is what you pay:

  • 7'x14' Utility Trailer: $40/day
  • 7x14 Dump Trailer 14k: $80/day
  • 24ft Enclosed Trailer: $95/day

No delivery surcharge, no pickup fee — no hidden fees of any kind. See our full published rate guide for week and month pricing. The price you book is the price you pay.

Who This Works Best For

Pickup-only rental is a great fit if you already have a tow vehicle with the right hitch and you're comfortable making a short trip to Eagle Mountain. It tends to work well for:

  • Local moves within Utah County where you're already driving a truck or SUV
  • Contractors and tradespeople who pass through the area regularly
  • Weekend project haulers who want to grab a trailer and go
  • Anyone who wants a firm, published price with no surprise charges at return

Booking Is Still Fast, Even Without Delivery

Not offering delivery doesn't mean we make you jump through hoops to get a trailer. We're open 24/7, and booking happens entirely online through our instant booking system. Reserve your trailer, show up at your scheduled time, hitch up, and go. If you'd rather talk it through first, you can also call (385) 269-0712.

What to Check Before You Book

  • Your vehicle has the correct hitch: 2" ball for the utility trailer, 2-5/16" ball for the enclosed and dump trailers
  • Your vehicle is wired for the matching plug (flat-4 for utility; round 7-pin for enclosed and dump)
  • You have a plan for both the pickup and the return trip
  • Your rental window (day, week, or month) matches how long you actually need the trailer

If a fee-free, no-surprises rental sounds like what you've been looking for, you can check availability and book your trailer right now through our online system, or call ahead if you have questions about hitch compatibility or trailer specs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is delivery available if I pay extra?
No. We do not offer delivery in any form. All rentals are pickup and return at our Eagle Mountain, UT location.
Are there any hidden fees I should expect at pickup or return?
No. All of our prices are published upfront, and because we don't offer delivery, there's no delivery fee added to your total either.
What hitch do I need to tow the enclosed trailer?
The 24ft enclosed trailer requires a 2-5/16 inch ball hitch and a round 7-pin plug connection.
Can I book a trailer outside of normal business hours?
Yes. We're open 24/7 and booking is handled through our instant online system, so you can reserve a trailer any time, day or night.
