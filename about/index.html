Meet Ed & Crystal — Owners & Your Neighbors

We're Ed and Crystal, and we've called Eagle Mountain home since 2017. Like a lot of our neighbors, we've watched this community grow fast — and we kept running into the same headache: when you needed the right trailer to move dirt, gear, or toys, good options were slim. That's exactly why we started Eagle Mountain Trailer Rental LLC, right here in our own backyard.

As a local family — truck owners and small-business operators ourselves — we knew the frustration firsthand. If you needed a heavy-duty dump trailer for a weekend landscaping project, or a secure, spacious enclosed trailer to haul a vehicle, you were usually stuck driving all the way down the valley or dealing with a big, impersonal national chain: long lines, worn-out equipment, and rigid hours that never seem to match a real workday.

So we built something different — founded on reliable, commercial-grade trailers and straightforward, neighborly service. Whether you're a local contractor needing extra capacity for a job site, a family moving across town, or a neighbor tackling a big backyard cleanup, we're here to help. We make booking seamless, offer flexible options, and keep our trailers clean, rugged, and ready — and you'll always reach a real person, the two of us, not a call center.

We aren't just renting out trailers; we're helping our neighbors build, move, and grow this community we love — one reliable haul at a time. Thanks for supporting a local, veteran-owned, family-run business.
