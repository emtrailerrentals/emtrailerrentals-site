Dump Trailer FAQ

What size dump trailer do I need for a landscaping job?
Our 7×14 dump trailer holds approximately 5–7 cubic yards of loose material. For a typical yard cleanup, debris removal, or landscaping rock delivery in Eagle Mountain or Saratoga Springs, one trailer load is usually enough for a half-day job.
What hitch do I need for the dump trailer?
A standard 2" ball hitch and Round 7 Pin plug connection. Most full-size trucks and SUVs are already equipped — check your owner's manual or hitch rating before renting.
