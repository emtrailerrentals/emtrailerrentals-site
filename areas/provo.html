Frequently Asked Questions — Provo Trailer Rental

How do I get from Provo to Eagle Mountain for trailer pickup?

Two easy routes: (1) US-89 north through American Fork to Eagle Mountain — scenic and fast. (2) I-15 north to Lehi, exit on SR-68 west. Both are about 25 minutes depending on traffic.

Is this a good option for Provo students moving apartments?

Absolutely. Our 24ft enclosed trailer at $85/day is the most cost-effective way to move an apartment — much cheaper than hiring movers, and you can do it on your own schedule. Book online, pick it up, load up, and return it the same day.

Do you rent dump trailers for Provo landscaping projects?

Yes — the 14k dump trailer at $80/day is perfect for rock, soil, sod, and debris hauling anywhere in Utah County including Provo. Multiple trips are easy since it dumps hydraulically in seconds.

What's the difference between a utility and enclosed trailer for a Provo move?

The utility trailer is open — great for items that don't need weather protection like furniture covered with moving blankets. The enclosed trailer is fully enclosed, lockable, and weather-proof — better for electronics, boxes, and overnight security if your move takes more than one day.
